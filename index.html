Center for Sexual Crimes Justice

A human-rights focused NGO dedicated to legal advocacy, research, and justice for victims of sexual crimes.

Who We Are

Iustitia is a non-governmental organization committed to strengthening victim-centered justice in cases of sexual crimes. Our work focuses on legal research, advocacy, public awareness, and institutional accountability.

Our Mission

  • Promote justice and dignity for victims of sexual crimes
  • Advocate for legal reforms aligned with international standards
  • Strengthen institutional response and accountability
  • Raise public awareness through education and research
